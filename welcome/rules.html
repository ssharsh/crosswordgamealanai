How to play and rules for the crossword game

1. Our alan ai integration is fully responsive, if you ask something it will respond to you. You could also check the readme file for more information.

2. To play our crossword game press the tile on where you wnat the letter to be placed.

3. If you press toogles answer, it will give you all the answers to the crossword puzzle.

4. You can ask alan ai for hints and answers and it will give to you. You just have to say give me a hint/answer for whatever number and it will give it to you.

5. You can also engage the challenge mode by asking alan ai to start the challenge.
